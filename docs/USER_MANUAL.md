## Getting Started

SkillSwap is a peer tutoring platform that connects students who need help with students who can help. Whether you're struggling with AP Calculus or want to share your Spanish skills, SkillSwap makes it easy to find and schedule tutoring sessions with your classmates.

### System Requirements
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection
- School email address for registration

---

## Creating an Account

1. Go to the SkillSwap homepage
2. Click **Sign Up** in the top right corner
3. Fill in your information:
   - Full name
   - School email address
   - Password (at least 6 characters)
   - Grade level
4. Click **Create Account**
5. You'll be automatically logged in and taken to your dashboard

**Note:** Only students with valid school email addresses can register.

---

## Finding a Tutor

### Browse Tutors

1. Click **Find Tutors** in the navigation bar
2. You'll see a list of available tutors

### Search and Filter

Use the search and filter options to narrow down results:

- **Search**: Type a skill name (e.g., "calculus", "spanish")
- **Subject Filter**: Select a specific subject area
- **Availability**: Filter by days/times they're available
- **Rating**: Sort by highest rated tutors

### View Tutor Profiles

Click on a tutor's card to see their full profile, including:
- Bio and background
- Skills they can tutor
- Availability schedule
- Reviews from other students
- Session history and statistics

## Booking a Session

### Request a Session

1. From a tutor's profile, click **Request Session**
2. Select:
   - Skill/subject you need help with
   - Preferred date and time
   - Duration (30, 45, or 60 minutes)
   - Location (library, online, etc.)
3. Add any notes about what you need help with
4. Click **Send Request**

### Session Status

Your sessions can have these statuses:

- **Pending**: Waiting for tutor to accept
- **Scheduled**: Confirmed and on the calendar
- **Completed**: Session finished
- **Cancelled**: Session was cancelled

### Managing Sessions

From your dashboard:
- View all your upcoming and past sessions
- Cancel sessions (with reason)
- Reschedule by cancelling and rebooking
- Mark sessions as complete

## Messaging

### Start a Conversation

1. Go to a user's profile
2. Click **Message**
3. Type your message and hit Send

### Message Inbox

Access your messages from the **Messages** link in the navigation:
- View all conversations
- See unread message count
- Search through messages

### Tips for Messaging

- Introduce yourself and explain what you need help with
- Be specific about times you're available
- Confirm meeting details before the session
- Be responsive to messages

---

## Leaving Reviews

After completing a session, you can leave a review for your tutor:

1. Go to **My Sessions**
2. Find the completed session
3. Click **Leave Review**
4. Rate 1-5 stars
5. Write your feedback
6. Submit

### Review Guidelines

- Be honest and constructive
- Focus on the tutoring experience
- Mention specific things that helped
- Keep it appropriate and respectful

## Becoming a Tutor

### Enable Tutoring

1. Go to **Edit Profile**
2. Scroll to **Skills I Can Tutor**
3. Add the subjects you can help with
4. Set your availability schedule
5. Save your profile

Once you add tutoring skills, you'll appear in search results and students can request sessions with you.

### Managing Tutor Requests

When students request sessions:
1. You'll see pending requests on your dashboard
2. Review the request details
3. Accept or decline the request
4. If accepted, it becomes a scheduled session

### Best Practices for Tutors

- Respond to requests promptly
- Be punctual for sessions
- Come prepared with materials if needed
- Follow up to make sure students understood

## Managing Your Profile

### Edit Profile

1. Click your name in the top right
2. Select **Profile** or **Edit Profile**
3. Update any information:
   - Profile picture
   - Bio/about me
   - Grade level
   - Skills offered and wanted
   - Availability

### Change Password

1. Go to **Settings** or **Edit Profile**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter current password
4. Enter and confirm new password
5. Save

### Privacy

Your profile is visible to other students on the platform. Your email is not displayed publicly.

## For Administrators

### Admin Dashboard

Administrators have access to additional features:

1. **User Management**: View, edit, or suspend user accounts
2. **Session Overview**: Monitor all platform sessions
3. **Review Moderation**: Approve or remove inappropriate reviews
4. **Reports**: View platform statistics and generate reports
5. **Audit Logs**: Track all administrative actions

### Accessing Admin Panel

1. Log in with an admin account
2. Click **Admin** in the navigation
3. Use the sidebar to navigate between sections

### Common Admin Tasks

- **Suspend a user**: Users > Find user > Change status to Suspended
- **Remove a review**: Reviews > Find review > Delete
- **View stats**: Dashboard shows key metrics
- **Export data**: Reports > Export to CSV

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

- Make sure you're using the correct email
- Check that Caps Lock is off
- Try resetting your password
- Clear browser cookies and try again

### Not Seeing Tutors

- Make sure you've selected a valid search term
- Check that filters aren't too restrictive
- Try clearing all filters

### Session Not Showing Up

- Refresh the page
- Check that the session was confirmed
- Look in different status tabs (Pending vs Scheduled)

### Messages Not Sending

- Check your internet connection
- Make sure the message isn't empty
- Try refreshing the page

### Other Issues

Contact the site administrator or use the **Help** page for additional support.
